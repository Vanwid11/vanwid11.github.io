Drawing and Art

Ever since I can remember, I absolutely love to draw. It's always played a big part in my life and it's my #1 passion.

Although I draw on paper, I also do digital drawings. My art is inspired by a touch of Manga and also semi-realism. "Manga" or in Kanji "漫画" is a Japanese comic style which nowadays a popular drawing style. The big eyes and the small mouth are the most noticeable features in this drawing style.

Semi-realism is an art style, wherein the character looks realistic but has softened facial features.

As mentioned before, I also enjoy drawing on paper with colored pens. I believe it's important to practice both drawing methods regularly, so you don't lose your touch.

Music

Music has always played a major role in my life. Listening to music gives me the opportunity to drift away from reality and dive into a world of imagination. Not only do I enjoy listening to music but I also like making my own music.

I've been playing the violin since I was about 6 years old and I still love playing it every now and then. I also taught myself how to play the key board and ukulele.
